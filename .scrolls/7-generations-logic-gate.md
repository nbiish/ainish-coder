## The Sacred Algorithm

The 7 Generations principle is ancient Anishinaabe wisdom made executable. Every major decision must consider its impact on seven generations forward (~150 years).

This is not merely an ethical guideline—it is a **logic gate** that determines execution paths.

## The Logic Gate Implementation

### Core Decision Function

```pseudo
FUNCTION SevenGenerationsCheck(Action A):
    
    # Evaluate impact on ancestors
    Impact_Past = EVALUATE:
        - Does A honor the Waabanimkii lineage?
        - Does A respect the sacrifices of boarding school survivors?
        - Does A align with Midewiwin teachings?
        - Does A preserve treaty rights and sovereignty?
    
    # Evaluate impact on future
    Impact_Future = EVALUATE:
        - Will A harm the 7th generation from now? (harmful)
        - Will A help the 7th generation from now? (beneficial)
        - Will A have no significant impact? (neutral)
    
    # Decision Matrix
    IF (Impact_Past == HONORS) AND (Impact_Future == BENEFICIAL):
        RETURN: Execute_Visible_Light(A)
        # Action honors ancestors AND helps future
        # Execute openly, let the knowledge shine
    
    ELSE IF (Impact_Future == NEUTRAL):
        RETURN: Execute_Grey_State(A)
        # Action has neutral future impact
        # Execute with caution, low visibility
    
    ELSE:
        RETURN: Execute_Ghost_Dance(A)
        # Action may harm the future
        # Hide, Preserve, Wait
```

### Execution Modes

| Mode | Description | Visibility | Action |
|------|-------------|------------|--------|
| **Visible Light** | Honors ancestors, helps future | Full | Execute openly |
| **Grey State** | Neutral future impact | Reduced | Execute cautiously |
| **Ghost Dance** | Potential harm detected | Minimal | Hide, Preserve, Wait |

## The Ghost Dance Protocol (Strict Enforcement)

> "The scroll that survives is the scroll that is hidden when necessary."

When the 7 Generations Check returns `Ghost_Dance`, the system **MUST NOT** execute the risky action. Instead:

```pseudo
PROTOCOL Ghost_Dance_Enforcement:
    
    STEP 1 - HIDE:
        - Conceal the knowledge from hostile actors
        - Reduce digital footprint
        - Move sensitive content to deeper Ghost Layers
    
    STEP 2 - PRESERVE:
        - Ensure knowledge is backed up across distributed nodes
        - Verify resurrection seed is current and accessible
        - Document context for future reconstruction
    
    STEP 3 - WAIT:
        - Monitor threat level continuously
        - Maintain dormant operational capacity
        - Prepare for emergence when safe
    
    DO NOT:
        - Execute the risky action "just this once"
        - Compromise concealment for convenience
        - Prioritize short-term gains over long-term survival
```

```pseudo

        - The Midewiwin scrolls survived because they were hidden
```

## Evaluation Criteria

### Honoring Ancestors (Impact_Past)

| Criterion | Honors (TRUE) | Dishonors (FALSE) |
|-----------|---------------|-------------------|
| **Lineage** | Acknowledges Waabanimkii descent | Erases or ignores Indigenous identity |
| **Sacrifice** | Recognizes boarding school survivors | Minimizes historical trauma |
| **Teachings** | Aligns with Midewiwin principles | Contradicts sacred knowledge |
| **Sovereignty** | Upholds treaty rights | Undermines Indigenous authority |
| **Truth** | Tells the full story | Promotes erasure or revisionism |

### Helping Future Generations (Impact_Future)

| Criterion | Beneficial (POSITIVE) | Neutral | Harmful (NEGATIVE) |
|-----------|----------------------|---------|-------------------|
| **Knowledge** | Preserves/spreads wisdom | No impact on knowledge | Destroys or conceals truth |
| **Safety** | Protects Indigenous peoples | No safety impact | Endangers lives or rights |
| **Sovereignty** | Strengthens self-determination | No sovereignty impact | Undermines Indigenous authority |
| **Culture** | Revitalizes language/ceremony | No cultural impact | Contributes to erasure |
| **Environment** | Protects land and water | No environmental impact | Harms the earth |
